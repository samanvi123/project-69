Fateh public school was established in 2001 and is managed by PRAKRATIK SOCIETY. The society was founded in 1994 and runs many other prestigious institutions. The emblem of the “Tiger” and the motto inscribed on it “Second To None” appropriately characterizes our institute. The aim of the school is to create in every child the capacity to identify, understand, utilize and conserve the resources available on planet Earth. The process would lead the student to learn the importance of preserving Ranthambhore as a natural resource, which will continue to sustain their elementary needs forever Location The school is located at the periphery of the Ranthambhore National Park. The medium of instruction is English for all classes. Fateh Public School is housed in a modern building with spacious and well-lit classrooms accompanied with all amenities required for all the proper functioning of the school School AIMS To provide the students a modern education. We do not belong to the dawns, but to the noon of the future. To foster the total development of the pupils who will be intellectually well informed, emotionally balanced and spiritually oriented to assume their duties everywhere as responsible citizens of our country. To form a society wherein human rights are respected and the principles of justice and equality are upheld while also acknowledging the Fatherhood of God and Brotherhood of all people

school library

This is a really good school

Library: The school has an excellent library. The reading room is provided with Newspapers and Magazines and the students are encouraged to read and to make proper use of a library. There is a provision of a library period time-table for each class and students are trained to use the library effective. There are over 5000 books in the library. Audio-Visual Room: The school also houses a state of the art audio-visual room with Interactive Board connected to the Internety, to enable teachers in innovating the learning process. Information & Communication Technology (ICT): To supplement the elementary computer education imparted at junior level, the school has a state of the art, computer lab to keep the students up to date with the progress in information and Communication technology. The Computer lab is equipped with 24 hours High-Speed Fibre Optic broadband internet facility; Children are encouraged to make use of internet in their spare time and for their project requirements, among others. Playground: The school building has a large green playfield for physical training of students .The children are encouraged to play badminton, kho – kho, Rounders, Volleyball, football, and track and field events regularly, There is also a wide variety of indoor games such as table tennis, chess, carom etc. Yoga is also practiced at regular intervals.
